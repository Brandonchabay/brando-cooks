Chicken Biyrani Recipe!

Description:

A flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with marinated chicken, spices, and basmati rice.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Basmati rice
  • Onions
  • Lemon Juice
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Yogurt
  • Spices (cumin, coriander, garam masala)
  • Fresh herbs (mint, cilantro)
  • Green chilies
  • Cooking ghee

Instructions:

  • Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, and spices for at least 1 hour.
  • In a large pot, heat ghee and saut√© sliced onions until golden brown.
  • Add marinated chicken and cook until browned.
  • Layer partially cooked basmati rice over the chicken.
  • Add fresh herbs, green chilies, and remaining spices.
  • Cover and cook on low heat until the rice is fully cooked and flavors meld together.
  • Serve hot with raita or salad.
