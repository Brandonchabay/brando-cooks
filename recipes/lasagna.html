Lasagna Recipe!

Description:

A classic Italian dish with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add marinara sauce to the beef and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, eggs, and half of the Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce. Layer with noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella cheese, and meat sauce. Repeat layers, ending with meat sauce and remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  8. Let lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
